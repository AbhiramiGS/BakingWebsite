BANANA MUFFINSJ!

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup oil
  • 1/2 cup curd
  • 2 ripened bananas
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract(optional)
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 2 cups powdered sugar
  • 2 cups maida

Method:

Preheat your oven to 350°F (180°C) and grease a 9-inch cake pan with oil or cooking spray. In a large mixing bowl, sift together the flour, sugar and salt. Take another bowl and mash the bananas. To that, add curd and oil and beat them well until its fluffy using a whisk. Add baking soda and baking powder to it. Pour the mixture to the dry inhredients ans stir gently in the same direction until the batter is smooth. Pour the batter into the prepared pan and bake for 30-35 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean. Remove the cake from the oven and let it cool completely before frosting or serving. Enjoy your delicious eggless banana muffins!
