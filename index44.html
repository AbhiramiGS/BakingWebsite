CHOCO VANNILA COOKIE!

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 granulated sugar
  • 3/4 cup unsalted butter
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract(optional)
  • Pinch of salt
  • 2 cups maida
  • 1 tbsp cocoa powder

Method:

Preheat your oven to 350°F (180°C) and grease a tray with oil or cooking spray. In a large mixing bowl, add the butter and stir well using a whisk until its fluffy. Grind the sugar and add it with the butter and extract. Mix them well, until the colour becomes light. Now sift together the flour and salt and make it a dough. Do not add water or milk. Now separate the dough into two parts. Add one part of the dough with cocoa powder. Create into shapes you like using a cookie cutter or by hand. Place the cookie into the prepared pan and bake for 10-15 minutes. Let it cool completely before serving. Enjoy your delicious choco vannila cookie!
